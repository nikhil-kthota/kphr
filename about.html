About Us





About KPHR Services

Established in 2021, we are a dedicated Food Safety Training company committed to building a Safer and more informed Food Industry. Since our inception, We have been Actively conducting Food Safety Awareness and Training programs for Individuals, Businesses, and Institutions across the sector.

Our goal is to Educate and Empower Food Handlers, Professionals, and Organizations with the Knowledge and Practical skills required to implement and maintain the Highest-standards of Food Hygiene and Safety.

Through Expert-led sessions, Up-to-date regulatory insights, and Hands on Training, We strive to create a culture of Safety, Compliance, and Accountability at every level of the Food supply chain.

Whether you're a Small Food business or a Large Enterprise, we are here to support your journey towards excellence in Food Safety.

Our Vision

To be the Leading force in building a Safer, Healthier World through Accessible, High-quality Food Safety Education and Training.

We Envision a future where every Food Handler, Business, and Institution understands and implements Global best practices in Food Safety-ensuring Public health, Customer trust, and Regulatory compliance.

To ensure the number of Trained Food Handlers/ Food Safety Supervisors throughout the country and every Food Handler to get Certified.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to Empower Individuals and Organizations with the knowledge and skills required to maintain the highest standards of Food Safety and Hygiene.

To train about or more than 5000 Food Handlers in Food Industry throughout the country and make sure that every Food Handler runs their Business maintaining all standards and precautions through the training.

We are committed to delivering expert-led, practical, and accessible training programs that help prevent Foodborne illnesses, support Regulatory compliance, and promote a culture of Safety across the Food Industry/Establishments.
